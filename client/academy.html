Non-fungible tokens or NFTs are those digital assets that symbolize real world objects like a piece of music, art, videos, text, games or even software. These NFTs are bought and sold online through cryptocurrencies. Their encryption is almost on the same lines of a cryptocurrency. These NFTs are present in the market place since 2014 but it is of late gaining attention of buyers and sellers in form of digital arts. NFTs are mostly unique – one of its own kind. These have unique identifying codes or unique identifiers. NFTs exist on blockchains which is a decentralized public ledger where all the records relating to a particular NFT is maintained. NFTs are non-fungible meaning that these are unique. What does this mean? This means that NFTs cannot be replaced with something else. Fungible items are equal in value. For example a rupee is fungible. One may exchange it for another one rupee anywhere in Bharat. A Bitcoin is also fungible and can be traded with another Bitcoin on the blockchain. Fungible nature of cryptocurrencies makes it a trusted means of conducting transaction on the blockchain. But one cannot trade an NFT with some other NFT because both of those will be unique and will be carrying different values. Besides, NFTs cannot be traded in fractions like Bitcoin or other cryptocurrencies. NFTs are bought and sold in its whole unit. One cannot buy or sell a fraction of an artwork, a music, a video or a game. This is one important difference that NFTs have with their crypto assets counterparts and that is the reason they have been classified as non-fungible. NFTs have their own digital signatures and that makes them unique. It can be compared with an autographed or signed artwork of an artist. Such physical objects will be unique and so are these NFTs. Due to this unique identifier every NFT is different and is one of its own kind. This is the reason that they cannot be exchanged or traded with any other NFT. We can understand this from a real life example. The video ‘Nyan Cat' was sold for almost 0.6 Million US Dollars whereas another artwork ‘Everydays - The First 5000 Days' was sold for 69 Million US Dollars. Now, these two NFTs cannot be exchanged or traded with one another, though both of them are NFTs. Hence all NFTs are unique and non-fungible. Also, at one point in time one NFT will have only one owner, unless and until there are exceptions applied. here.

Digital wallets are those wallets that store digital assets. Digital wallet can be defined as a software programme that stores and allows transactions of digital assets. These wallets are protected through a private key and this key ensures ownership of the crypto assets in the wallet to its owner.
This private key allows the owner of the digital wallet to access his digital assets. This key uses sophisticated encryption and is integral part of cryptocurrencies and its security makeup. It consists of a series of alpha-numeric characters that makes it very difficult for a hacker to actually break it. This key is very crucial in depositing and withdrawing crypto assets from the digital wallets. Upon initiating a transfer from the wallet, wallet software creates a digital signature with this private key while processing the transaction. This helps in maintaining the security and in generating a valid signature. Even the slightest of the alteration in the transaction will change the digital signature that will cancel the transaction and then a fresh validation will be needed. It is extremely important that this private key is kept secretly and safely. If one loses this private key, one loses all his access to his crypto assets.
These wallets help in updating the transactions on the blockchain's distributed ledger. Digital wallets help in completing the creation, minting, purchase and sale process of your NFTs and other crypto assets very quickly and record them in real time. Digital wallets make it easy to securely manage and maintain one's crypto portfolio. Digital wallets can be used to store all sort of crypto assets including NFTs.
Cryptocurrency is a high value target for hackers and therefore it is important to store crypto assets safely. You should choose a wallet based on your requirements and you must handle it carefully to prevent any loss of data. Wallets are stores of your crypto assets to facilitate transactions and therefore its proper maintenance and increased awareness towards its security is a must.
