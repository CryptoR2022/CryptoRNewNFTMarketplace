INTRODUCTION

This is an endeavour from CryptoRNFT.Com to build a strong community that can inspire, stimulate and empower creators as well as collectors. NFT is a new phenomenon and hence it is to be nurtured carefully for its growth and development. This growth is to be achieved by creating a beautiful NFT ecosystem that respects all who are associated with NFTs. These could be, of course, the creators of NFTs who are manifesting their brilliance before the world, or these could be the collectors who are appreciating and supporting such fine illustrations and taking a call to purchase the same. We have introduced a third angle to it by involving experts to rate NFTs being listed on our marketplace. This will help and support buyers in taking their decisions to buy NFTs.

COMMUNITY

CryptoRNFT.Com has developed a Telegram channel and Twitter channel xxxxxxxxxxxxx which will be the binding force between the creators, collectors and rating experts. Here, all three can interact with each other and can share their work and experience to develop and grow the community. This community will preserve the tradition and will pave way for innovation and expansion. Community members will have mutual respect and regard for each other. Anyone who is serious to contribute in the domain of non-fungible token in any way can be part of this community.

CREATORS

Creators are a very important link in this chain of creating and selling of NFTs. They create and generate NFTs, and put it for the display before the audience. Audience enjoys their skills and appreciates their artful manifestations. Those who fall in love with a piece of art become buyer of that NFT. We endorse that creators should be given freedom to express their creativity so that their ideas come out without any restriction and binding. They can rightfully express their vision before the audience. At the same time, creators must understand that everything comes at a cost and their freedom to creativity is no exception. Creators have to ensure that-

A) They do not upload somebody else‚Äôs copyrighted work or proprietary work,
B) They present beautiful work for the audience and their work should comprise of quality work,
C) They avoid duplicating their work to maintain the uniqueness of the work. However, work representing a series or variation is acceptable,
D) Their work should not spread hatred and violence against anyone or any group. The purpose of art is to spread the message of love and peace. Hence, their work should imbibe this spirit and should not present anything that could be against a community, religion, nation, society, ethnicity and culture,
E) They do not present anything which could be illegal, illegitimate, fraudulent, criminal or anything against the law of the land,
F) There should not be any display or promotion of any pornographic and/or sexually explicit material and/or content, and
G) They do not endorse, promote and glorify any activity that is self-harming and suicidal in nature.

We believe that creators‚Äô community is highly responsible community and is aware of its duties and commitments as a creator.

Creators retain and maintain all legal rights, including copyrights and trademarks of their original work. They can reproduce, distribute, exhibit and make derivative work of their piece of work on CryptoRNFT.Com marketplace.

COLLECTORS

An artist‚Äôs work is incomplete unless there is a collector for that work. Collectors are those important links in the chain where the art culminates. Collectors appreciate arts and their creators and as a mark of respect decide to invest in that piece of art. This way they support, promote and help their favourite artist and artwork. Anyone who is engaged in buying, purchasing, selling, re-selling, exchanging and possessing the artwork is a collector. Artists on the NFT marketplace are revolutionising the arts industry and collectors are the flag bearers of this revolution.

As a collector you own your rights on the NFT that you have bought. The NFT is available on the blockchain and you can display, share, trade and resell that piece of NFT as per your wish and choice in the secondary market.

However, you must remember that you cannot claim copyrights, trademarks, or other intellectual property rights as a collector unless specifically mentioned by the Creator.

We want those collectors in our community who have an eye for detail and who can truly value and cherish any piece of work. It totally depends on collectors‚Äô community how they shape up the marketplace by their presence and contribution to this new form of art.
